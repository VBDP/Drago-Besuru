To install the groan tube on your avatar
1: Drag the "XeviansGroanTube" prefab onto your avatar
2: Unpack completely
3: Drag "GroanTubeAnchor" to the cooresponding bone on your avatar i.e. Right Hand
4: Position the anchor as needed
5: Copy the "Reference Controller" to your FX controller using AV3 Manager
6: Copy the "Reference Parameters" to your avatar paremeters
7: Create a toggle in your menu for "GroanToggle"
8: Disable the groan tube
9: Enjoy!
